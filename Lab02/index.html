Chicken Biryani

Recipe Creator

Pabitra Devi BK(MOM)

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 4 tablespoons biryani masala
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • Salt, to taste
  • 4 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 green cardamom pods
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 cup fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 cup mint leaves, chopped
  • Saffron strands, soaked in 1/4 cup warm milk
  • 3-4 tablespoons ghee
  • 3 cups water

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice in water for 30 minutes. Drain and keep aside.
  2. Marinate chicken pieces with yogurt, biryani masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, salt, half of the chopped mint and coriander leaves. Let it marinate for at least 1 hour.
  3. Heat oil in a large pot. Add cumin seeds, bay leaves, cardamom pods, cloves, and cinnamon stick. Saut√© for a minute.
  4. Add sliced onions and fry until golden brown.
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste and fry until raw smell disappears.
  6. Add marinated chicken and cook until chicken is half done.
  7. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  8. Layer the chicken with half-cooked rice, remaining mint and coriander leaves, saffron milk, and dots of ghee on top.
  9. Add water, cover and cook on low heat until the rice is fully cooked and chicken is tender.
  10. Let it sit for 10 minutes after cooking. Gently fluff and mix the biryani before serving.
  11. Serve hot with raita or salad.

Why This is My Favorite Recipe

This Chicken Biryani recipe holds a special place in my heart because my mom always used to cook it for special occasions. The aromatic spices, tender chicken, and fluffy rice combine to create a luxurious and flavorful dish. It's a bit more involved than some recipes, but the end result is always worth the effort and brings joy to any gathering.
