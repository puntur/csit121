Egg Curry

Recipe Creator

Subarna Amatya (MOM)

Ingredients

  • 6 boiled eggs
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, minced
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 2-3 green cardamom pods
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • 1 cup water

Instructions

  1. Peel the hard-boiled eggs and make small slits in them.
  2. This will allow the eggs to absorb the flavors from the curry.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© the onions until they are golden brown.
  4. Add the ginger and garlic and fry for a minute.
  5. Stir in the pureed tomatoes and cook until oil separates from the masala.
  6. Add curry powder and salt, and mix well.
  7. Reduce the heat to low, and carefully add the whisked yogurt to the pan.
  8. Stir continuously to prevent the yogurt from curdling.
  9. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and coriander powder.
  10. Mix well and cook until the masala starts releasing oil.
  11. Add water to the masala and bring it to a boil. Adjust salt.
  12. Gently place the eggs in the curry. Let them simmer in the curry for about 5-7 minutes.
  13. Serve hot with rice or bread.

Why This is My Favorite Recipe

This egg curry recipe holds a special place in my heart because my mom always used to cook it for me every weekend. The blend of spices, the creamy texture, and the aroma make it a comforting and delicious dish. It's simple to prepare and never fails to bring joy to the dining table.
